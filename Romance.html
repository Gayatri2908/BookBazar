It Ends with Us

"It Ends With Us" by Colleen Hoover is a contemporary romance novel that follows Lily Bloom, who falls in love with a charming neurosurgeon named Ryle Kincaid. However, as their relationship progresses, Lily must confront the cycle of domestic abuse in her life, influenced by her past and her complex relationship with her first love, Atlas Corrigan. The story explores themes of love, resilience, and the difficult choices that shape our lives.

It Starts with Us

"It Starts With Us" by Colleen Hoover is a sequel to "It Ends With Us," continuing the story of Lily Bloom. After her separation from Ryle, Lily reconnects with her first love, Atlas Corrigan. As they navigate their renewed relationship, Lily deals with the challenges of co-parenting her daughter with Ryle while striving for a healthier and happier future. The novel delves into themes of healing, second chances, and the enduring power of love.
