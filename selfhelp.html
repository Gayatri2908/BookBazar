Atomic Habits

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear is a self-help book that delves into the science of habit formation and how small changes can lead to significant personal and professional growth. The book provides practical strategies for building good habits, breaking bad ones, and mastering the tiny behaviors that lead to remarkable results. Clear emphasizes that making small, incremental improvements every day can compound into extraordinary outcomes over time.

What on earth am I here for?

What on Earth Am I Here For?" by Rick Warren is a spiritual self-help book that explores life's purpose from a Christian perspective. It provides a framework for understanding one's life mission based on biblical principles, emphasizing five key purposes: worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and mission. The book guides readers to discover their purpose and live a meaningful, purpose-driven life aligned with God's plan.
